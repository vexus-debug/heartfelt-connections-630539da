

# Vista Dental Care - Complete Website Plan

## Overview
A comprehensive, professional dental clinic website with a clean, medical-grade aesthetic using **deep navy blue**, **teal green**, and **white**. The design will follow the modern, friendly layout from your reference image while reflecting Vista Dental Care's brand as trustworthy and patient-centered.

---

## Color Theme
- **Primary**: Deep Navy Blue (trust, professionalism)
- **Secondary**: Teal Green (health, freshness)
- **Accent**: White (cleanliness, clarity)
- **Soft backgrounds**: Light blue-gray and mint tints for sections

## Key Features

- **Responsive Design**: Works beautifully on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- **Navigation**: Sticky header with dropdown menus for services
- **Contact Form**: Validates inputs, shows confirmation message
- **Promo Banner**: Visible across site during promotion period
- **Smooth Animations**: Subtle scroll animations for modern feel
- **Social Links**: Instagram @vista.dcs, phone, email

## Design Elements (Inspired by Reference)
- Rounded corners and soft shadows
- Friendly imagery placeholders of smiling patients
- Icon-based service cards
- Decorative organic shapes as background accents
- Clear call-to-action buttons throughout
